Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world, action role-playing game that allows the player to control one of four interchangeable characters in a party. Switching between characters can be done quickly during combat, allowing the player to use several different combinations of skills and attacks.It allows the player to explore the world freely at their own pace. It has an age rating of 12 but no age verification process, even a child could sign up. It gives the story and the graphics of this game is phenomenal and has an interesting storyline.

VALORANT

Valorant is an online multiplayer computer game, produced by Riot Games. It is a first-person shooter game, consisting of two teams of five, where one team attacks and the other defends. Players control characters known as 'agents', who all have different abilities to use during gameplay.
